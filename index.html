Project Euler 030

Digit n Powers

Surprisingly there are only three numbers that can be written as the sum of fourth powers of their digits:

1634 = 14 + 64 + 34 + 44

8208 = 84 + 24 + 04 + 84

9474 = 94 + 44 + 74 + 44

As 1 = 14 is not a sum, it is not included.

The aim is to find the sum of all the numbers that can be written as the sum of nth powers of their digits. Begin by entering a number.
